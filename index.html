Home/RAG Development

R.A.G. Development

Retrieval-Augmented Generation improves large language models by combining them with external, up-to-date data sources. Instead of relying only on static training data, RAG models retrieve relevant information from a knowledge base to generate more accurate, contextual responses.

01

Indexing Stage

We prepare your external knowledge base for efficient retrieval — collecting data from documents, databases, or APIs; cleaning and chunking text into meaningful segments; and generating vector embeddings that capture semantic meaning.

02

Retrieval Stage

When a user query is received, we preprocess and convert it into an embedding. The system performs similarity searches across the vector database to find the most relevant chunks using hybrid or re-ranking methods.

03

Fusion Stage

The retrieved data snippets are intelligently merged with the user's original query to create a rich, context-aware prompt. This augmented input is then passed to the LLM, enabling it to generate more precise and factually consistent responses.

04

Generation Stage

We validate and refine model outputs to ensure they remain accurate and traceable to your source data. This includes optional response verification, output ranking, and feedback-based fine-tuning for continuous improvement.

Home/Fine-Tuning

Fine-Tuning

Fine-tuning takes a pre-trained, general-purpose model and further trains it on a smaller, task-specific dataset to adapt it for a specialized purpose. It leverages the model's existing knowledge and adjusts parameters to improve performance for your unique needs.

01

Problem Definition & Model Selection

We start by defining the exact task and performance objectives for your AI solution. Once the scope and success metrics are clear, we identify and select the most suitable pre-trained model for your purpose.

02

Data Preparation & Structuring

We collect, clean, and preprocess a high-quality dataset tailored to your use case. This includes annotation, normalization, and splitting into training, validation, and testing sets for balanced, reliable development.

03

Configuration & Fine-Tuning

We adjust the model architecture for your task, integrating methods like LoRA or PEFT to optimize performance. Our team carefully tunes hyperparameters like learning rate, batch size, and epochs for optimal results.

04

Validation & Deployment

We rigorously test the fine-tuned model using relevant performance metrics before deployment. Once integrated into your environment, performance is continuously monitored to ensure reliability.

Home/Gen AI Diagnostic

Generative AI Diagnostic Review

We evaluate your deployed AI system to understand how well it performs in real-world use. We test with diverse queries, identify hallucinations, check RAG pipeline completeness, and assess prompt handling, safety, and alignment.

01

Problem Identification

We begin by identifying the exact goals and expected behaviors of your deployed AI system. We outline test scenarios, failure modes, and performance indicators needed for a thorough diagnostic review.

02

Data Structure & Evaluation

We examine how your AI uses internal and external knowledge sources, reviewing RAG pipelines, document coverage, and data structuring. This includes checking embeddings, chunking strategies, retrieval accuracy, and content freshness.

03

Behavioral Testing & Stress Analysis

We stress-test your application under real and adversarial conditions, evaluating prompt handling, hallucination rates, and reasoning quality. We test for missing retrievals, broken chains of thought, and prompt injection vulnerabilities.

04

Validation & Improvement

We validate performance using targeted benchmarks and scenario-based testing. Once results are analyzed, we deliver a structured improvement plan detailing fixes for RAG gaps, tuning needs, and safety hardening.
